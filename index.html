Privacy Policy

Your data stays with you

Local Processing Only

PDF Mate processes all PDF files entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third-party API. We do not have access to your PDF content, file names, or any metadata.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share your personal information or PDF files. The extension operates entirely offline after installation. The only network requests made are for license verification (if you choose to activate Pro features).

Chrome Storage

We use Chrome's local storage API to save your license key and preferences. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Help Center

Frequently asked questions

How do I split a PDF into specific page ranges?

Select the "Split" tool, upload your PDF, then choose "Custom Ranges" mode. Enter ranges like "1-3,5,7-9" to extract specific pages or ranges into separate files.

Can I rotate pages in both directions?

Yes! Select the "Rotate" tool, choose your desired angle (90°, 180°, or 270°), then select either Clockwise or Counter-Clockwise direction.

How do I delete multiple pages at once?

In the "Delete" tool, switch to "Enter Pages" mode and type page numbers or ranges (e.g., "1,3,5-10,15"). This is much faster than clicking individual pages for large documents.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your PDFs are never uploaded to any server. PDF Mate works offline once installed.

How do I activate Pro features?

Click the "Upgrade" button or go to Settings → License Key. Purchase a license from our Gumroad store and enter your license key to unlock unlimited pages and all features.
